Where Everybody Belongs

    The school band room is right next ot the cafeteria. The teacher teaches jazz and band. Jazz Band emphasizes instruction in advanced techniques of jazz instrument playing. These include skills in tone, intonation, rhythm, tempo, dynamics, articulation, harmony, phrasing, style, and improvisation. Put simply, a school band is a musical ensemble made up of students around the same age and skill level. Your school will likely offer a beginning band class in which all students are learning their instruments together.
